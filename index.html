Publications

Here is a selection of my recent publications. For the full list, please check my Google Scholar profile.

Innovative 3D-printed surfaces for efficient water harvesting from air
Furkan Turan Koyun, Sema Sabur, Güldemet Başal, Hüseyin Günerhan
International Journal of Clothing Science and Technology, Vol. 37, No. 4, pp. 698-708, 2025   (Journal Article)
journal page

The purpose of this study is to develop nature-inspired 3D surfaces for atmospheric water harvesting. Cylindrical, conical, and tree-shaped protrusions were 3D-printed and coated with hydrophilic nano-scale particles and fibers using electrospraying techniques. Wettability patterns were varied, and the water collection rates and capacities of these surfaces were evaluated.

Revolutionizing Healthcare: The Role of Generative AI and Large Language Model Chatbots in Medical Applications
Koyun, F. T., Çalışkan, T., Onan, A., Korkut, S. Ö.
(Work-in-Progress), 2025

An ongoing research exploring the integration of generative AI and LLM-based chatbots into medical applications, aiming to enhance diagnostic support, patient engagement, and healthcare accessibility.

3D Printed Smart Surfaces for Water Harvesting from Air
Koyun, F. T., Başal, G.
International Congress on Sustainability and Technological Developments in Textiles, 2023   (Conference Presentation)

Presentation on innovative 3D-printed smart surfaces designed for efficient atmospheric water harvesting, focusing on material design and surface engineering approaches in textiles.

Nanocomposite Structure for Oil/Water Separation
Oral, N., Koyun, F. T., Başal, G.
V. International Ege Composite Materials Symposium, 2021   (Conference Presentation)

This study presents a composite membrane system made from cellulose acetate nanofibers and PNIPAM hydrogel particles via electrospinning and electrospraying. The developed multilayer membrane efficiently separated water from oil-water mixtures using gravity-driven filtration.
